Network Cable Management

SJS Datacomm designs and installs various cable management solutions that provide structural support to organize, store and secure your cabling. We have a highly trained technicians who are dedicated solely to providing real-time response to customers’ requests for service.

 

We take great pride in providing structured cabling solutions that come with extensive manufacturer warranties as well as our unmatched “Lifetime Quality Workmanship Warranty”.

Wire Management Systems include horizontal & vertical cable management for your free standing equipment racks or cabinet enclosures. This helps organize cables and cords throughout your network.

Cable Pathway Systems include cable runway (ladder rack/cable tray) used for horizontal, vertical and backbone pathways and can be installed within and outside of your data room, under raised floors and above acoustic ceilings.

Fiber Duct Raceway Systems, made of non-metallic wiring duct (UL 94V-0 Flammability Rated and Zero Halogen Content for Safety) are used to route, protect and store fiber cabling. Fiber duct is primarily installed in data centers, central offices, and wiring closets and provides a dedicated path for mission critical network cabling.
